# Wi-Fi Lens — Privacy Policy

*Last updated: June 12, 2026*

Wi-Fi Lens ("the app") is designed with privacy as a core principle. This policy explains what data the app handles and what it does not.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** Wi-Fi Lens does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data, usage data, or device identifiers. The app has no backend, no analytics SDK, no advertising, and no third-party trackers.

## What the camera does

The app uses your device's camera solely to scan printed Wi-Fi credentials (router labels, Wi-Fi cards). All image processing and text recognition happens on-device using Apple's Vision framework. No images are saved, uploaded, or shared.

## Wi-Fi connection

When you tap **Connect**, the app uses Apple's `NEHotspotConfiguration` API to request a network join. The credentials never leave your device; the system handles the connection.

## Children's privacy

The app is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.
