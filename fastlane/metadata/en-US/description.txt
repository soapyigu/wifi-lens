Wi-Fi Lens turns your camera into the fastest way onto a new network. Point it at a router label, a Wi-Fi card, or any printed credentials, and Wi-Fi Lens reads the network name and password in real time, then connects with a single tap.

How it works
• Point the camera at printed Wi-Fi credentials
• On-device text recognition extracts the network name and password
• Tap Connect — iOS handles the secure join

Why you'll like it
• Pure on-device processing — credentials never leave your phone
• No account, no tracking, no ads, ever
• Works with most home and small-business router labels

Wi-Fi Lens is built for guests, families, and travelers tired of squinting at tiny labels and typing long passwords by hand.
